The tool I offer you on the Programs & Progress page allows you to automatically count your repetitions (push-ups, pull-ups, handstand push-ups ...) and your hold times (handstand, planche, front lever‚Ä¶) using your device's camera. It's an innovative tool that allows you with unprecedented ease to track your sessions automatically thanks to computer vision.

It was designed to provide objective feedback on your practice, without approximation, and meets the need to no longer have to tediously record the content of each session manually in a text tool that becomes obsolete!

You can record your sessions in two different ways:

  • You can easily record your session manually by entering exercises and repetitions in the table I provide, then publish your sessions.
  • Or record your session even more easily with the automated AI tool that counts your repetitions / holds. Simply, at the end of each exercise or set of exercises, click on "Save Session" so your session is stored in your account!

Step 1 ‚Äì Launch the Tool

Login block for the training tool

Step 2 ‚Äì Exercise Selection and Optional Weight Configuration

Here are the simple steps to follow:
  1. Allow camera access and position it so your entire body is visible. I will show you examples of how to use the application below.
  2. Select the exercise you want to perform from the dropdown menu.
  3. In case of a weighted exercise, fill in the weight field by entering the weight in kg.

Step 3 ‚Äì Exercise Execution

For the motion sensor to work optimally, make sure your body is well highlighted.

Once the exercise is finished, a message indicating that the set is complete appears and you have the option to save your session (in which case the system adds your set or series of sets to your history) or start a new set of the same exercise!

‚ÑπÔ∏è For the following exercises: Push-ups, Pull-ups and Dips, for the exercise to be considered finished, at the end of the movement (either in a suspended position with arms in the air for pull-ups, or in the high position for push-ups or dips) you must remain still for 2.5 seconds! The system then finalizes the set and you can save your session smoothly.
