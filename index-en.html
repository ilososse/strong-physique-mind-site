Site Objective

The purpose of this site is to provide you with both a theoretical and practical approach to bodyweight training. Published articles will give you all the knowledge you need to progress under the best conditions, and the training tool will allow you to measure your progress. This site also offers the opportunity to connect with a community of athletes who can share their training sessions to motivate each other. Finally, a Leaderboard has been set up to bring out the best in you and challenge you week after week!
