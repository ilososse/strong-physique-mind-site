Calisthenics, Psychology & Personal Development

This site is designed by a calisthenics practitioner with over 10 years of experience, passionate about health, psychology, and personal development.

The goal: offer simple training programs to implement for everyone: from beginner to advanced users. But also provide resources on the theory behind the practice: the biomechanics of movements, the health benefits, and how practicing this sport can accelerate your personal development.

Site Spirit

What differentiates this site is that it is infused with the perspective of an experienced athlete who brings a scientific and analytical approach to this discipline. Over my 10 years of practice, I have managed to achieve the most emblematic strength skills: from the human flag to the planche, passing through the front lever. I now wish to share this expertise with you, with an approach that goes beyond just a how-to guide for this sport, but encompasses with equal importance the psychological aspect and the mindset needed to achieve your goals. And as the saying goes: "A sound mind in a sound body", some of the posts will focus on the health benefits of movements and will offer reflections on dietary advice and lifestyle tips to reach your full potential. This site will offer you:

  • Examples of structured training programs for three levels;
  • An amazing performance tracking tool to observe your own progression;
  • A Feed system allowing you to find inspiration and motivation in the training sessions shared by your peers;
  • A ranking of the best athletes of the week;
  • Psychological reflections on discipline, identity, and motivation.

Site Objective

The purpose of this site is to provide you with both a theoretical and practical approach to bodyweight training. Published articles will give you all the knowledge you need to progress under the best conditions, and the training tool will allow you to measure your progress. This site also offers the opportunity to connect with a community of athletes who can share their training sessions to motivate each other. Finally, a Leaderboard has been set up to bring out the best in you and challenge you week after week!

Reflections & Analysis

Alongside training, I also share more personal texts: ideas, analyses, observations on discipline, the relationship with failure and self-development.
